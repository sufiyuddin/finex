# Privacy Policy for Finex

Finex is an offline expense and budget tracking application. Your privacy is important to us.

## Information Collection and Use
Finex does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All financial data entered by users remains on the device.

However, the app uses third-party advertising services.

## Third-Party Advertising
Finex uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data such as:
- Advertising ID
- Device information
- Interaction with ads

This data is collected to provide personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with Google policies.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy
